What's new?

The 2024 Audi Q8 e-tron is a pinnacle of electric luxury SUVs, featuring a striking design, an impressive EPA-estimated range of up to 285 miles, and a 2023 IIHS TOP SAFETY PICK+ award.

With a powerful 402 HP and a rapid 0-60 mph time of 5.4 seconds with Boost Engaged, the Q8 e-tron delivers a dynamic and thrilling electric driving experience, supported by two electric motors.
